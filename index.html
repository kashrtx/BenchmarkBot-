About BenchmarkBot

BenchmarkBot provides accurate, up-to-date information about AI language models, helping you make informed decisions about which models to use for your specific needs.

Our data is gathered from multiple trusted sources including LiveBench.ai, official benchmarks, leaderboards, and provider documentation, and is updated in real-time to ensure you have access to the most current information.

We're committed to transparency and accuracy in our reporting, providing a comprehensive view of the AI landscape without bias toward any particular provider or model.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, unbiased, and up-to-date information about AI models to help users make informed decisions.
